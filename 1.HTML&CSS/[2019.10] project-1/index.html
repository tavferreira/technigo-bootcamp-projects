6 Things anyone with a bathroom can´t live without

Get a Friday feeling. Make the whole week melt down...
Pour a glass of wine. Immerse yourself in a bubble bath and let the candles and the insence wash away your stress of the week.

Grab your glass of wine. Inhale. Exhale. Enjoy!

Glass of wine →

After a long day at the office, nothing beats a long hot bath and a glass of wine. Red, white, bubbly as your bath, it doesn't matter!

Candles →

Because: Sweden. Right?
No seriously, can you imagine how life would be without those candles staring at you every morning? Here's a hint: BO-RING!

Rose petals →

Petals, petals, petals. Petals everywhere! Can you come up with anything less necessary in a bathroom? Exactly, neither did we!

Rubber duck →

All bathrooms should have this companion who is going to be at your side through thick and thin, good days and bad days. The sheer presence of this little buddy gives a soothing touch to your bathroom.

Incense →

Aroma therapy – a great way to help you maximize the bath experience. A nice incense that fills the bathroom with a calming fragrance will do the trick.

Bath bubbles →

Release your inner child. Grab your rubber duck and fill up the tub with bubbles.
